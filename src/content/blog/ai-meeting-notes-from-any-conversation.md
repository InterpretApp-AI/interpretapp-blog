Taking notes during a meeting means you're not fully present in the conversation. Interpret AI solves this by generating structured notes automatically from your live transcript.

## How It Works

Start a transcription session during your meeting — from the browser or the **desktop app**. Interpret AI transcribes everything in real time with **automatic speaker differentiation**, so you know exactly who said what.

Then, with one click, the AI generates structured notes from the full transcript.

## Note Formats

Interpret AI offers multiple note formats, each designed for a different use case:

### Meeting Notes
A structured summary with agenda items, key discussion points, decisions made, and action items — the format most people need after a standard team meeting.

### To-Do Lists
Extracts tasks and action items from the conversation, organized with dependencies and ownership. Great for sprint planning or project check-ins.

### Q&A Logs
Captures the key questions raised during the meeting and the answers given. Perfect for training sessions, onboarding calls, or product demos.

### Follow-Up Email Drafts
Generates a professional email summarizing what was discussed and the next steps — ready to send to participants or stakeholders who couldn't attend.

## The AI Chatbot

Beyond structured notes, Interpret AI includes an **AI chatbot** that understands the full context of your transcript. You can ask it:

- "What were the main decisions made in this meeting?"
- "Summarize what Sarah said about the timeline"
- "List all the action items mentioned"
- "What questions were left unanswered?"

The chatbot provides suggested prompts based on your transcript content, so you don't have to think of what to ask.

## Review with Audio Playback

After the meeting, open the transcript in the **editor**. Click any word to hear that exact moment in the recording — powered by **word-level timestamps**. This is invaluable when the AI summary captures a decision but you want to hear the nuance of how it was discussed.
