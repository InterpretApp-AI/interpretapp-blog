Meetings move fast. Between action items, decisions, and side discussions, it's easy to miss something. Real-time transcription captures everything so you can focus on the conversation instead of your notes.

## Starting a Live Transcription

### From Your Browser

Go to [interpretapp.ai](https://interpretapp.ai), open the **Transcribe** page, and start a session. Grant microphone access and start talking. The transcript appears in real time.

### From the Desktop App

If your meeting is on a video call, the **Interpret AI desktop app** captures audio directly from your computer. This means it works with any conferencing tool — no plugins, no integrations, no browser extensions. Just start the desktop app and it transcribes whatever audio is playing on your machine.

## Real-Time Speaker Differentiation

Interpret AI **automatically identifies different speakers** and labels them in the transcript. In a meeting with four people, you'll see each person's contributions clearly separated and attributed. No manual setup required — the AI detects speaker changes on its own.

This is especially valuable for:

- **Meeting minutes** — know exactly who said what
- **Action items** — attribute commitments to the right person
- **Legal or compliance contexts** — maintain an accurate record of who made which statements

## Live Translation

If your meeting includes people who speak different languages, enable **live translation**. Each participant can view the transcript in their preferred language from 70+ options. They can also toggle **Live Audio** to hear a translated version spoken by AI.

Share the **broadcast link** with remote participants and they'll see the live translated transcript on their phone or laptop.

## After the Meeting

Every meeting transcript is saved automatically with full audio. Open it in the **transcript editor** to:

- **Play back the recording** synced to the transcript — click any word to jump to that exact moment
- **Word-level timestamps** make it easy to find the part where a specific decision was made
- **Edit the transcript** for accuracy — fix names, technical terms, or misheard words
- **Translate** the full transcript into additional languages for international team members
- **Share** the transcript with people who couldn't attend

## Who Uses This

- **Remote teams** who need meeting notes without a dedicated note-taker
- **International teams** who meet across language barriers
- **Managers** who need to review what was discussed and decided
- **Consultants** who need accurate records of client conversations
