Interpret AI has launched a new feature enabling **live audio streaming** in its Broadcast Room. This addition complements the platform's existing real-time transcription and translation capabilities, allowing users to hear meeting audio synchronized with displayed text.

## How It Works

The Live Audio Streaming functionality works across Speaker Rooms and Broadcast links. When users select a language for text display, the system attempts to provide corresponding translated audio when accessible.

## Key Benefits

- **Accessibility:** Provides essential auditory engagement for visually impaired users and those preferring audio-based learning
- **Contextual Understanding:** Speaker tone and emphasis add nuance beyond text transcription
- **Multitasking Support:** Listeners can review notes or step away while maintaining engagement through audio
- **Language Learning:** Hearing spoken language alongside real-time translations aids language acquisition

## How to Use

1. Join a Broadcast Room
2. Locate the **Live Audio** toggle button
3. Select your preferred language
4. Enable the stream

The toggle can be disabled at any time.

## Under the Hood

The platform leverages **WebSockets** for real-time data transfer and the **Web Audio API** to deliver low-latency audio playback directly in the browser — no plugins or downloads required.
