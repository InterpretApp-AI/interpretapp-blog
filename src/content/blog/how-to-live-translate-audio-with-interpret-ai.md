Whether you're traveling to another country, an international student listening to a lecture, or providing live captions for an audience — Interpret AI makes real-time translation accessible to everyone.

## What Interpret AI Can Do

- **Live AI translation** in 70+ languages
- **Transcript storage** so you can review sessions later
- **Zoom and Microsoft Teams integration**
- **Real-time audio translation** with shareable caption links
- Accessible on **desktop and mobile**

## Getting Started in 3 Steps

### 1. Create an Account

Head to [interpretapp.ai](https://interpretapp.ai) and sign up. You'll get 100 free credits to start — no credit card required.

### 2. Start Transcribing

Navigate to the **Transcribe** page and start a session. Select your source language and any target language for live translation.

### 3. Share or Review

Share your broadcast link with your audience so they can follow along in their language. All sessions are saved to your **Documents** section for future reference.
