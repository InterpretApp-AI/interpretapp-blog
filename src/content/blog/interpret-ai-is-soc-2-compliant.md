## What Is SOC 2?

SOC 2 (Service Organization Control 2) is a security framework developed by the American Institute of Certified Public Accountants (AICPA). It evaluates how a company handles customer data based on five trust principles:

1. **Security** — Is the system protected against unauthorized access?
2. **Availability** — Is the system reliably available for use?
3. **Processing Integrity** — Does the system process data accurately and completely?
4. **Confidentiality** — Is confidential information properly protected?
5. **Privacy** — Is personal information collected, used, and retained appropriately?

SOC 2 certification means an independent auditor has verified that Interpret AI meets these standards.

## Why It Matters for Transcription

Transcripts contain the actual words people say — often in contexts where confidentiality is critical:

- **Legal firms** transcribing depositions and client calls
- **Healthcare organizations** documenting patient interactions
- **Financial services** recording compliance-sensitive discussions
- **Enterprises** transcribing board meetings and strategy sessions

Without SOC 2 compliance, you're trusting a vendor with sensitive data based on their word alone. With SOC 2, you have third-party verification.

- **Private storage** — Transcripts are accessible only to the account owner unless explicitly shared
