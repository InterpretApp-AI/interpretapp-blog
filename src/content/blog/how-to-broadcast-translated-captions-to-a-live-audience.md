Running an event with a multilingual audience? Instead of hiring interpreters for every language, you can broadcast live translated captions that audience members read on their own phones.

## How It Works

1. **You speak** — Interpret AI captures your audio and transcribes it in real time
2. **AI translates** — The transcript is translated into 70+ languages simultaneously
3. **Audience follows along** — Each person opens a link on their phone and selects their language
4. **Live audio** — Audience members can optionally listen to AI-spoken translated audio

The whole setup takes about two minutes.

## Step-by-Step Setup

### 1. Start a Broadcast Session

Log in at [interpretapp.ai](https://interpretapp.ai), go to the **Transcribe** page, and start a session. You can also use the **desktop app** to capture audio from any source on your computer.

### 2. Get Your Broadcast Link

Once your session is live, copy the broadcast link. This is the link your audience will use to follow along.

### 3. Share with Your Audience

Display the link on a screen, print it on programs, or share it via text/email before the event. You can also generate a QR code for easy scanning.

### 4. Your Audience Picks Their Language

When audience members open the link, they select their preferred language from 70+ options. Captions appear in real time in their chosen language. They can also toggle **Live Audio** to hear the translation spoken aloud.

## Speaker Differentiation

If multiple people are speaking at your event — a panel discussion, a Q&A session, a worship service with different leaders — Interpret AI **automatically identifies and labels each speaker** in the transcript. Your audience sees who is saying what, even in translation.

## After the Event

The full transcript is saved with **word-level timestamps** and audio. You can:

- Share the transcript with attendees who couldn't make it
- Open the **transcript editor** and click any word to hear that exact moment
- Translate the transcript into additional languages for post-event distribution
- Edit the transcript for accuracy before sharing
