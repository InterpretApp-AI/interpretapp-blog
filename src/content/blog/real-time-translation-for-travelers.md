You're at a market in Tokyo, a café in Paris, or a bus station in Istanbul. Someone is speaking to you and you need to understand — right now, not after typing into an app.

## How Interpret AI Helps Travelers

Open [interpretapp.ai](https://interpretapp.ai) on your phone, start a session, and point your microphone at the conversation. Interpret AI:

1. **Transcribes** the speech in its original language
2. **Translates** it into your language in real time
3. **Identifies speakers** so you can follow who's saying what

No tapping a button for each phrase. No switching between language pairs. Just continuous, live translation.

## Compared to Google Translate

Google Translate's conversation mode requires you to **tap a button every time someone speaks** and only handles **one language pair at a time**. If you're in a group where people speak different languages, you're constantly switching.

Interpret AI runs continuously — it captures everything and translates it all at once. You can even share a **broadcast link** with the people you're traveling with so everyone sees translations on their own phone.

## Save and Review Later

Every conversation is saved to your **Documents**. Back at the hotel, you can:

- Re-read the conversation with the vendor who gave you directions
- **Play back the audio** and click any word to hear it again with **word-level timestamps**
- **Translate** the transcript into additional languages
- **Share** the transcript with friends or travel companions

## Works Offline-Ready

The **desktop app** captures audio from any source on your computer — useful for translating travel videos, language learning content, or pre-trip research.
