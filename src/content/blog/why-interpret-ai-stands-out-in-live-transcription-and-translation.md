How does Interpret AI compare to competitors like Otter.ai, Notta AI, and Limitless AI? We ran tests across six key use cases to find out.

## The Use Cases We Tested

1. Broadcasters needing translated captions for a live audience
2. Travelers requiring live conversation translation
3. Deaf users wanting real-time captions
4. Content creators seeking accurate transcription and translation
5. Marketing agencies translating documents
6. Business employees needing meeting transcripts

## Transcription Accuracy

We ran a ground truth test comparing transcription quality side-by-side.

> **Otter.AI** had the least impressive result — leaving words uncapitalized and adding an erroneous `.you.` at the end.

Interpret AI performed well with only minor omissions, while Limitless AI dropped one word. Overall accuracy was strong across the board, but presentation and consistency matters.

## Live Translation

We tested Spanish translations and analyzed quality using ChatGPT as a neutral evaluator.

> Interpret AI "retains the 'AI' in 'traducción en vivo de IA,' which maintains the intended meaning of 'Live AI Translation.'"

Notably, **Otter.AI and Limitless.AI don't offer live translation at all** — putting Interpret AI in a category of its own for multilingual use cases.

## Broadcast Support

Only **Interpret AI** supports live audio of broadcasts spoken by AI. This is a unique capability not matched by any of the three competitors tested.
